WEIGHT TRAINING

Weight training is an integrated activity. The trainee is called upon to overcome resistance in many ways. The choice of the program and how to perform the exercises is a complex process based on the trainer's abilities, the information he gathers from the environment, and the experience he / she can get through the workout. An ally in this difficult effort is the proven training techniques and the little secrets of practical application. In our gym in Paros you will find specialised trainers to help you achieve your goals.
